About us

Welcome to Lifeflow, where we are committed to making a life-saving difference through blood donation. Founded in 2017, our organization has a rich history of connecting generous donors with those in need, creating a close-knit community of compassion and support.

Our Mission

    At Lifeflow, our mission is simple yet profound:
  • To promote voluntary blood donation as an act of altruism.
  • To connect donors with recipients, providing hope and lifesaving support.
  • To uphold the highest standards of safety, professionalism, and ethics in our work.

Why Choose us

  • We have a strong history of successfully organizing blood donation drives and facilitating life-saving connections.
  • We are more than just an organization; we are a family united by the shared goal of saving lives.
  • We believe in transparent and accountable practices, from donor registration to blood distribution.

Terms & condition policy

1. Reservation Policy:

We value your commitment to saving lives through blood donation. To ensure a smooth and organized process, please note the following reservation policy:
  • Appointments can be made online or by phone.
  • Walk-in donors are welcome, but those with reservations will be prioritized.
  • Please arrive on time for your appointment. Late arrivals may need to reschedule.
  • We understand emergencies can arise; kindly inform us in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment.

2. Cancellation Policy:

We appreciate your understanding in the event that you need to cancel your blood donation appointment:
  • If you must cancel, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Last-minute cancellations may affect the availability of blood for those in need.
  • To cancel or reschedule, contact our team via our website or phone.

3. Privacy Policy:

Protecting your privacy is a core principle of our service:
  • We collect, use, and store your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience; you can manage your cookie preferences.
Please ensure that these policies are adapted to the specific needs and legal requirements of your website and its users.
