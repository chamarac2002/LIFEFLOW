Donation Procedure

  • Blood donation is a kind deed that can save lives. Depending on the location and organisation the blood donation process may differ. However, the method often entails the following steps:
    •     1. Registration: You will be required to complete an application and give permission for a donation.
        2. Medical check-up: A doctor will examine you and take your temperature, blood pressure,pulse, and blood     levels. They will also inquire about your medical history and lifestyle.
        3. Donation: A phlebotomist will take a unit of blood from your arm (often 350ml or 450ml).

  • It's crucial to remember that you should make sure you meet the organization's eligibility requirements before giving blood. Both the donor and the beneficiary can profit greatly from blood donation.
